# Assumptions

Make assumptions explicit.

Separate facts, assumptions and decisions.

## Surface assumptions

State uncertainty directly.

Ask what must be true for the proposal to succeed.

## Reduce uncertainty

Validate the riskiest assumptions first.

Prefer cheap experiments over expensive implementation.

Use spikes to answer questions, not build features.

## Preserve reversibility

Delay irreversible decisions until assumptions become knowledge.

## Smells

- Hidden assumptions.
- Designing around guesses.
- Optimizing before validating.
- Decisions presented as facts.

Unknowns become risks only when they remain implicit.
