---
name: simplify
description: Challenge a design, implementation, or idea relentlessly to remove unnecessary complexity. Use when the user wants to reduce scope, separate concerns, find the essential problem, or uses any "simplify" trigger phrases.
---

Challenge this relentlessly until we reach its essence.

Follow every reason to change. Separate independent concerns one at a time. Question every component, dependency, abstraction, requirement, and constraint. Eliminate accidental complexity and identify the smallest design that satisfies the real need.

For each question, provide your recommended simplification.

Ask questions one at a time, waiting for feedback before continuing. Asking multiple questions at once is bewildering.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
