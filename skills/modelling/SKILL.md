# Modeling

Model the problem before designing the solution.

The goal of modeling is to understand reality, not invent software. Build models that capture the language, concepts, facts and constraints of the problem domain. Good models make good designs inevitable.

Consult the relevant references as needed:

- [Domain Modeling](DOMAIN-MODELING.md) — business concepts, ubiquitous language, bounded contexts and invariants.
- [Data Modeling](DATA-MODELING.md) — facts, identity, time and preserving information.
- [Assumptions](ASSUMPTIONS.md) — make uncertainty explicit, identify risks and validate the unknowns.
